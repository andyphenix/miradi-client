Miradi Share www.miradishare.org
Learn how to share your project with your colleagues within your program and beyond.

Miradi Share is a new cloud-based software system that enables conservation practitioners, managers, and funders to design, manage, monitor, and learn from collections of related conservation "projects" that make up a conservation "program"

Miradi Share provides a powerful suite of tools that build on and extend the functionality provided by CMP Open Standards for the Practice of Conservation and Miradi Desktop Software.
