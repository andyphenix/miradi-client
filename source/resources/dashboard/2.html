2. Plan your actions and monitoring

Once you have described the basic parameters for your project, the next step is to design the specific strategies you will undertake to achieve conservation. In particular, this step involves developing your Action Plan that you will use to record your project's goals, strategies, and objectives. It also includes developing the Monitoring Plan that you will use to track your progress and developing your Operational Plan that will specify the resources you need and risks you should consider. Finally, it involves compiling all your work from Steps 1 (Conceptualize) and 2 (Plan) into your Strategic Plan.
