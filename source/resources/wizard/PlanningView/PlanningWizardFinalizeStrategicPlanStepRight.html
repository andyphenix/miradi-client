You can use the <Progress Report> side tab in the lower panel to update the status of a given strategy:
  • Scheduled for Future Implementation: Strategy is not yet started
  • Major Issues: Ongoing, but has major issues that need attention
  • Minor Issues: Ongoing, but has minor issues that need attention
  • On Track: Ongoing, generally on track
  • Completed: Successfully accomplished
  • Abandoned: No longer relevant or useful
You can use the <Who and When> side tab in the lower panel to assign who is responsible for undertaking and leading each strategy or action. Dates can be set in the Workplan View.
