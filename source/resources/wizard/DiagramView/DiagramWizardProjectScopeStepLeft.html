The first step in building a conceptual model is to put your project scope to one side (usually the far right) of your workspace.

To insert your scope, click on the <Insert Target> button in the Control Bar. The software will add a target to the page with a Project Scope box around it. You can select and drag the target to wherever you want it to be. You can then double-click on the green Project Scope box to either review the scope you entered earlier or enter a new scope statement.

Hint: In the current version of the Miradi software program, the scope is not a separate entity in the diagram, but instead is drawn automatically around all of your targets. You thus add your scope by adding a target to a blank diagram.
