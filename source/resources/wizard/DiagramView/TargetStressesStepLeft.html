Some conservation planning methodologies, such as The Nature Conservancy's CAP Process, call for identifying the stresses that affect your conservation targets. If you want to use stresses, double click on a target, and click on the <Stresses> tab in the target properties window. Click on the buttons to create a new stress, create a stress from an existing stress, or create a stress from one of your KEAs. For each stress, you can then fill in the ID, name, and details fields. You can also click the <Stress Visibility> side tab and then the <Show Stress on this page> button to display a stress in a box on the current conceptual model page, or the <Hide Stress on this page> button to hide it.

Hint: Stresses are displayed on the diagram as blue boxes. You can position them on top of links from other factors to targets, but you cannot link to them directly. When you click on a stress, its corresponding target will also be selected. Likewise, when you click on a target, its corresponding stresses will be selected.
