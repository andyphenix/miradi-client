In addition to the individuals on your project team and the organizations they represent, you may also want to consider the other organizations that you are partnering with on your project.

To record these other partner organizations, click on the <Other Orgs> tab and then click the <Create Organization> button to create a record for your first partner. You can then fill in the appropriate fields for this organization in the bottom panel. Fields include:

  • Organization Name - The name of the partner organization including its full name, and if needed, a shorter acronym or abbreviation.
  • Role(s) - The role or roles that this organization plays in the project.
  • Org Contact: Given Name / Surname / Email / Phone - Information about the individual who represents the organization.
  • Comments - Any comments about this partner organization.
When you are done, you can click the <Create Organization> button again to create a new record and add other partner organizations as necessary. In this view, as in all Miradi tables, the panel at the bottom displays the information for the table row that is highlighted on top. Click the <Next> button when you are done entering all other partner organizations. If necessary, you can also click the <Delete Organization> button to delete the highlighted row of the table.

Hint:Although Miradi 3.0 does not support direct links between the team and other organization tabs, future versions of Miradi will enable you to link these two tabs.
