A resource is a person or group that is needed to complete an activity/method or a task. Miradi will be able to help you build a detailed budget for your project by calculating the cost for the estimated work that has been assigned to each resource.

Hint:To calculate a Daily Rate for Project Resources that are paid an hourly rate, you can multiply the hourly rate by the number of hours in the average/standard work day. For Project Resources that are paid a yearly salary, it is most common to divide the salary by 240, which is approximately how many work days there are in a standard American work year.
