Just as each strategy will have one or more activities associated with it, each monitoring indicator will also have one or more methods associated with it.

In the Planning View, click on the <Work Plan> tab and select an indicator that you want to focus on. Think about the specific methods that you will have to undertake to measure this indicator. Click on the <Create...> button and the click <Create Method> to add a new method and then type the name of the method in the label box in the bottom panel. If necessary, you can have multiple methods for each indicator. Repeat for other indicators as needed.

If necessary, you can also highlight a method and click on the <Create...> button and then click <Create Task> to add tasks under this method. (If you are really detail oriented, you can even create sub-tasks and sub-sub tasks under a task.)
