Once you have completed your ratings for all four criteria, the software automatically calculates the target-threat rating for this target and threat combination. You should check whether you agree with this rating. If it does not make sense, go back and adjust the ratings for the specific criteria.

Fill in the following fields that apply to the Target-Threat Rating:

  • Comments: Discussion about the Target-Threat Rating.
  • Threat Rating Evidence: Your confidence rating in the Target-Threat Rating.
  • Evidence Notes: Discussion of evidence that supports the the Target-Threat Rating.
