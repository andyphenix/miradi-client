About the More Information Pages

The More Information Pages are context sensitive to the view that you are currently in. As the name implies, they provide additional material describing the specific step(s) in the adaptive management cycle as well as links to further reading on the topic.

CMP Open Standards

Miradi software is being developed to assist conservation practitioners to go through the adaptive management process outlined in the CMP's Open Standards for the Practice of Conservation.  You can download a copy of the latest version at:

CMP. 2008. Open Standards for the Practice of Conservation, v 2.0.
     http://www.conservationmeasures.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/CMP_Open_Standards_Version_2.0.pdf

Many of the CMP members also have their own version of the standards as well as specific guidance materials for many of the steps as shown below.
