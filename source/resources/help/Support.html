Help with Miradi Software

To get answers to questions or problems, we recommend the following steps:

  1. "How-To" Answers in Miradi: Once you have opened a project in Miradi, use the Step-by-Step instructions in the green panels at the top of the screen. You can use the <Step-By-Step> menu or the <Instructions> button found at the bottom of many screens to jump to the relevant spot in the interview sequence.

  2. "More Info" in Miradi: For content oriented questions, you can also consult the blue <More Info> button at the top of each view..

  3. "How-To" Answers Online: Go to the Community page at: miradi.org/community for access to YouTube Tutorials, Miradi User Group Webinar recordings, and Supporting Documents. You may also want to consult the list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) at: miradi.org/faqs.

  4. If you have obtained a license from the Miradi.org website, you can also e-mail support questions to: support@miradi.org.
    We will endeavor to answer your questions as soon as possible.
