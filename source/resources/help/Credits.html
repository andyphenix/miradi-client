Credits

Miradi software is being developed as a joint effort between the Conservation Measures Partnership and Benetech. It is the result of work of dozens of different organizations and hundreds of people over many years. As a result, keeping this credits list up-to-date has become a virtually impossible task. So recognizing that we are undoubtedly not including everyone who should be mentioned (and that people's organizational affiliations may have changed), key people and organizations involved in the development of Miradi include:
