Many threats affect more than one target. To link your threat to another target, select the direct threat, hold the <Shift> key and then select the desired target. Then click on the <Add a Link> button (the arrow). You can also link direct threats to each other using the <Add a Link> button (the arrow).

Hint:The best way to link two factors in Miradi is to select the first one, hold the shift key, select the second one, and then hit the <Add a Link> button (the arrow) in the legend. This method avoids the need to scroll through a lengthy list of all factors in the system.

If desired, you can also show a stress between a direct threat and a target. On a link connecting a direct threat with a target, double-click or right-click and choose Properties, and enter the stress name where prompted.
