Some conservation planning methodologies, such as The Nature Conservancy's CAP Process, call for identifying the stresses that affect your conservation targets. If you want to use stresses, from the Edit / Preferences menu, select the <Threat Rating View> tab. In this tab, change the Threat Rating Mode field to "Stress Based Threat Rating Mode."

Now, if you double click on a target, you can now click on the <Stresses> tab in the target properties window. Click on the buttons on the right to create a new stress, clone an existing stress, or create a stress from one of your KEAs. For each stress, you can then fill in the ID, name, and details fields. The ratings fields will be used later.

Hint:If you are using stresses, once you have added direct threats to your diagram, you can then click on the link between the threat and the target to display your stresses in the diagram. Check the Linked to Threat field for each stress that you want to appear in that link.
