Results chains are a key tool for developing strategies.To begin, click on a strategy in the diagram. Next, click on the <Create Results Chain> button (available either on the toolbar or by right-clicking the mouse). This will create a new tab in the diagram window that has the strategy and linked factors on it.

You can include multiple factors in your new results chain by selecting them before clicking the <Create Results Chain> button.

If there is a chain icon shown in a strategy hexagon, you can view the existing results-chain by either clicking on the appropriate tab or by clicking on the strategy and then using the <Show Results Chain> button (available either on the toolbar or by right-clicking the mouse).
