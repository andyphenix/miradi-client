Place Project Scope on Diagram

The first step in building a conceptual model is to put your project scope to one side (usually either the far right or top) of your workspace.

To insert your scope, click on the <Insert a Target> button in the legend. The software will add a target to the page with a Project Scope box around it.

You can select and drag the target to wherever you want it to be. You can then double-click on this box to either review the scope you entered earlier or enter a new scope statement.
