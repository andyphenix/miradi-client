Navigation Hints

To add a new target or other factor, click the appropriate button on the control bar, use the drop down menu, or use the left mouse button. The new factor will appear on the page. To select a factor, single click on it. To reposition a factor, first select it and then use the mouse or arrow keys to move it wherever you like.

Double clicking on a factor will bring up a dialog box in which you can name the factor, change its type, link an indicator or objective to it, or type a comment. Factors with comments linked to them will have a blue triangle in the corner.

To link factors click on the first, hold the shift key, and then click on the second. Then click the arrow button to create the link. Or if you select a factor and then add a new factor, they will be automatically linked.

To switch between conceptual models and results chains use the tabs in the upper left and the page listings in the top of the control bar. The zoom-in and zoom-out buttons can be used to decrease or increase the size of the view. Other more advanced tasks are described in subsequent wizard pages.
