The final step is to determine the desired future status  for each indicator.

For a selected indicator, fill in the following fields:

  • FS Rating:  What rating category you want the indicator to be in.
  • FS Date: The date of the desired future status.
  • FS Label: Brief description of the desired future value of the indicator.
  • FS Text: Full description of the desired future value of the indicator. 

    • Miradi will automatically display the FS Label, along with an icon, in the cell of the indicator ratings table corresponding to the desired future status.
