A work plan shows the specific activities / methods and tasks that you need to undertake to implement your strategic and monitoring plans.

The workplan also enables you to develop a budget for your project (in the future, it will also enable you to manage actual expenditures). The budget shows the costs and funding sources for the activities/methods and tasks that you need to undertake to implement your strategic and monitoring plans.

Miradi currently supports planning and budgeting in 3-month quarters; it will eventually support other time frames. As a general rule, you should have a detailed work plan for the coming 2 to 4 quarters and a rough plan for the years after that.
