Click on the <Scope> tab and add your project's vision statement to the Vision Text field. Review the criteria for a good vision statement and revise your statement as necessary.

If desired, you can also click on the <Location> and the <Planning> tabs to add additional detail about your project.

In addition, Miradi also has tabs for key CMP organizations that contain additional organizational specific fields. These fields are especially for key data that will be used in reports and uploads to organizational databases. Contact your organization's Miradi lead for more information.

If your organization would like to develop its own fields, contact Miradi staff at info@miradi.org.
