Navigation Hints for the Planning View

Click on the radio buttons in the Control Bar to select standard planning views. Alternatively, click on the Create Custom button to create your own combination of rows and columns.

Miradi creates a tree based on the relationship between goals, objectives, and strategies/indicators in your conceptual models and/or results chains. Clicking on the + and - symbols enables you to expand or collapse different parts of the hierarchy. Selecting a row in the table will allow you to bring up the bottom panel and edit the information for that row.

Much of the information in the planning view is automatically generated from the diagram. To create or delete a goal, objective, or strategy, you have to go the diagram and use the properties dialogue for the relevant factor.
