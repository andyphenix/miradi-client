Although for some direct threats there may be only one contributing factor, for others, there may be many. The idea is to construct chains showing the sequence of indirect threats and opportunities affecting your threats.

You can add additional factors to the chain by using the <Insert Contributing Factor> button. You can add additional links by selecting one factor, holding the <Shift> key, selecting the second factor, and then clicking on the <Insert Link> button (the arrow).

You can also highlight a chain by selecting a direct threat factor, right (alt) clicking the mouse, and picking the <Select Factor Chain> command.
