The next step is identify your strategies. Strategies are linked to chains of factors showing the sequence of contributing factors affecting your direct threats and ultimately your targets.

To select chain that you want to focus on, select a factor in the chain, and the right (alt) click the mouse, picking the <Select Factor Chain> command. All factors and links in the chain should now be highlighted. (If you need to add or remove factors from the chain, hold the <Ctrl> key and click on the factors you wish to add or remove.)

Next, click on the <Brainstorm Mode> (available on either the toolbar or by right-clicking the mouse). This should hide all factors except the ones that you have selected.

Hint: A grey background on the diagram view means that you are showing only a selected chain in brainstorm mode and some factors are hidden.
