Indicators are linked to factors in a model. If you used the detailed method of viability analysis, you have already created indicators for your targets. The information on this page is not applicable for targets set to use the detailed method. However, you should create indicators for other factors, where appropriate.

To create a new indicator, double click on a factor and then click on the <Indicators> tab in the properties dialogue box. Click on the <Create Indicator> button at the side of the page. Fill in the following fields:

  • Indicator ID: The text that will appear in the indicator symbol on the diagram. Often a number (e.g., "3" or "17").
  • Indicator Name: The name of the indicator. Generally 2-5 words (e.g., Number of Sharks)

Click on the priority and status boxes to assign the appropriate level to each indicator. You can also fill out the following fields to describe the current status (CS) of an indicator:

  • CS Date: The date of the most recent indicator measurement.
  • CS Label: Brief description of the current value of the indicator or other short descriptor.
  • CS Text: Full description of the current value of the indicator.
  • Trend: Your assessment of the direction this indicator is heading.
  • Confidence: Your confidence in the source of the indicator rating.

    • To edit or delete an indicator, click on the desired row and then either edit the text in the box at the bottom or click on the <Delete Indicator> button.

    You can also view and edit all your project's indicators using the Indicators Only setting in the Planning View.
