The final step is to determine the desired future status for each indicator. Click on an indicator in the table and the bottom panel, scroll down below the indicator ratings to the desired future status section.

For the selected indicator, fill in the following fields:

  • Date: The date of the desired future status assessment.
  • Parameter: The desired future value or summary qualitative description of the indicator.
  • Rating: The rating (VG, G, F, P) that corresponds to the desired future status measurement.
  • Detail: Full description of the current value or qualitative description of the indicator.
  • Comments: Discussion about the desired future status.

    Miradi will automatically display the Future Status parameter, along with a blue oval icon, in the cell of the indicator ratings table corresponding to the desired future status.
